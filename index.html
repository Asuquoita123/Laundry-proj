    How it Works

    #1) SIGN-UP: Confirm your pickup address and create your account.

    #2) SCHEDULE: Make an order and choose a day/time for your
    pickup & delivery.

    #3) PICK-UP: Package your dirty laundry in 13 gal size trash bags
    and give to our driver.

    4) DELIVERY: Receive your freshly clean and folded laundry same
    day, next day or two days.
