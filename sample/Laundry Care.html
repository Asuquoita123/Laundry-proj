How it Works


#1) SIGN-UP: Confirm your pickup address and create your account.

#2) SCHEDULE: Make an order and choose a day/time for your pickup & delivery.

#3) PICK-UP: Package your dirty laundry in 13 gal size trash bags and give to our driver.

#4) DELIVERY: Receive your freshly clean and folded laundry same day, next day or two days.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost?
For standard, wash & fold service we charge a flat rate per bag. As much as you can fill! Included with the bag price are items needing special care; hand-wash, air-dry, stain treatment. We'll also hang and fold according to your preferences. Ironing is a separate fee of $3 per garment. Items that are not clothing (tablecloths, cleaning rags, etc.) are priced based on the quantity and other processing requirements. Contact our Sales Team for speciality pricing.
When is my laundry returned?
Our standard turnaround is 2 days and is included in our service (no extra fee). If you need it back sooner you can request same day / next day delivery. There is an additional mark up of 30% of your invoice for a faster turnaround.
How do I find out if you cover my area?
On the Home page or the Residential page, enter your address in the "enter pickup address" box. We'll check to see if our drivers serve your location and let you know right away!
How do I get started?
Setting up laundry service is easy! Once you've created your account, you can request your first pickup!
